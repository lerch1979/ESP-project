# HR-ERP Mobile App — User Guide

## Overview

The HR-ERP mobile app provides employee wellbeing tracking and support services through two integrated modules:

- **WellMind** — Daily mood tracking, assessments, and coaching
- **CarePath** — Employee Assistance Program (EAP) with case management and provider booking

## Getting Started

1. Open the app and log in with your company email and password
2. The **Dashboard** shows your key metrics and a Wellbeing card
3. Use the bottom tab bar to navigate between sections

## Navigation

| Tab | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Kezdőlap | Dashboard with stats and wellbeing overview |
| Hibajegyek | IT support tickets |
| Jóllét | WellMind + CarePath hub |
| Munkavállalók | Employee directory |
| Továbbiak | Settings, documents, notifications, more |

## WellMind — Lelki Egészség

### Daily Pulse (Napi Hangulat)
- **What**: Quick daily mood check-in (takes 30 seconds)
- **How**: Tap the emoji that matches your mood (1-5 scale)
- **Optional**: Add stress level, sleep quality, workload, and notes
- **When**: Once per day — get into the habit!

### Pulse History (Hangulat Előzmények)
- View your mood trends over 7, 30, or 90 days
- Bar chart shows daily values with 7-day moving average
- Anomaly warnings if a sudden drop is detected

### Quarterly Assessment (Negyedéves Felmérés)
- **What**: Burnout (MBI) and engagement (UWES) assessment
- **How**: Answer 30 questions on a 1-10 scale
- **When**: Once per quarter
- **Results**: Burnout score, engagement score, risk level (green/yellow/red)
- Auto-generated intervention recommendations if risk is elevated

### Interventions (Beavatkozások)
- View recommended wellbeing interventions
- Actions: Accept, Complete (with rating), or Skip
- Types: coaching, meditation, exercise, time off, training

### Coaching Sessions
- View scheduled and completed coaching sessions
- Rate completed sessions (1-5 stars) with feedback
- See action items and topics discussed

## CarePath — Gondoskodás (EAP)

### Create a Case (Új Ügy)
1. Select a service category (counseling, legal, financial, etc.)
2. Describe your issue (max 500 characters)
3. Set urgency level (low/medium/high/crisis)
4. Optional: Make the case anonymous
5. Submit — matched providers will be suggested

### My Cases (Ügyeim)
- View all your cases with status and urgency badges
- Filter by: All, Open, In Progress, Closed
- Tap a case to see full details, sessions, and bookings

### Provider Search
- Search by city or specialty
- Filter: counseling, legal, financial, crisis, family
- View ratings, distance, and specialties

### Book Appointment
- Select a provider
- Choose: in-person, video, or phone
- Pick available time slot
- Add optional notes
- Confirm booking

### Close a Case
- When resolved, close with satisfaction rating (1-5 stars)
- Add resolution notes

## Notifications (Értesítések)

Access from More > Értesítések. Types include:
- Pulse reminders
- Assessment due
- Intervention assigned
- Case updates
- Appointment reminders
- Coaching scheduled

Tap a notification to navigate directly to the relevant screen.

## Privacy & Confidentiality

- **Anonymous cases**: Your identity is hidden from the provider
- **Session notes**: Encrypted with PGP (not readable by HR)
- **Pulse data**: Only you and aggregated team metrics (min 5 employees) visible to managers
- **Crisis protocol**: Crisis-level cases alert HR for immediate support

## FAQ

**Q: How often should I do the Daily Pulse?**
A: Every workday. It takes 30 seconds and helps track your wellbeing.

**Q: Can my manager see my individual pulse scores?**
A: No. Managers only see anonymized team averages (minimum 5 people).

**Q: What happens if I select "Crisis" urgency?**
A: An immediate alert is sent to HR for emergency support. Only use for genuine emergencies.

**Q: Can I delete a case?**
A: Cases cannot be deleted but can be closed. This preserves audit trails.

**Q: How are providers matched?**
A: By category, language, specialty, location, and rating. Top 5 are shown.
