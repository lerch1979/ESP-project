## SLIDE 3: THE INSIGHT

# Housing Quality Predicts Everything

Our discovery: **accommodation quality is the strongest predictor of workforce stability** for blue-collar workers.

- Housing dissatisfaction → 1.8x job dissatisfaction (Borg & Kristensen, 2000)
- Poor housing → **2x turnover risk** (Evans et al., 2003)
- Housing improvement → 25-30% fewer workplace accidents (ILO, 2020)

**Nobody else is measuring this. We are.**

## SLIDE 5: HOW IT WORKS

# Automated Early Warning System

```
Employee fills daily pulse (30 seconds)
        ↓
Platform detects patterns across 6 data streams
        ↓
Automatic trigger fires when thresholds exceeded
        ↓
Support initiated: notification + referral + intervention
        ↓
HR/Manager receives action recommendation
        ↓
Loop closes: track outcome, adjust intervention
```

**From detection to support in minutes, not months.**
