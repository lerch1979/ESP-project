## The Problem

Blue-collar industries lose **€5,000-€15,000 per employee exit**. With 30-45% annual turnover, a 200-person company hemorrhages **€500,000+/year** — yet traditional HR tools (annual surveys, reactive EAP hotlines) were built for desk workers, not construction crews living in shared accommodation.

## Our Solution

A **proactive, data-driven workforce stability platform** that connects six data streams — daily mood, burnout assessments, overtime, housing quality, sick leave, and workplace conflict — to detect instability **weeks before crisis** and automatically initiate support.

| Module | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **WellMind** | Daily pulse surveys + quarterly MBI/UWES assessments + automated interventions |
| **CarePath** | EAP case management + provider network + crisis protocol |
| **Housing Stability Index** | Accommodation quality tracking correlated with employee wellbeing |
| **Predictive Analytics** | Overtime→burnout, sick leave triggers, conflict escalation, turnover risk |
